Chicken Al Pastor

Description

Chicken al pastor is a tasty dish made with tender chicken and pineapple. Toasty, spicy and sweet this recipe features skewered chicken and pineapple served on top of a base of corn tortillas.Chicken Al Pastor, the cousin to Pork Tacos Al Pastor, is 1000% crave worthy exploding with smoky, tangy, juicy charred chicken and caramelized pineapple. Al Pastor is traditionally cooked on a rotating spit crowned with pineapple, but is surprisingly easy to make by baking or grilling the chicken on skewers to achieve the same irresistible juicy caramelized crust. Serve up Chicken Al Pastor in tacos, burritos, bowls or salad along with Mexican Street Corn Salad, or Elote and Perfect Fruit Salad for a complete feast!

Ingredients

  • 4 dried guajillo chiles, stemmed and seeded
