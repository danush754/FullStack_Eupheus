Garlicky Shrimp Zucchini Boats

Description

Garlicky Shrimp Zucchini Boats are a delicious and healthy low-carb dish. Fresh zucchini halves are filled with succulent shrimp seasoned with aromatic garlic, herbs, and spices. Baked to perfection, these zucchini boats are a flavorful and satisfying meal option, perfect for those seeking a light and flavorful dinner.
